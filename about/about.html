About Us


            

            We are bulgarian highschoolers, studying in ELSYS (Electrical Systems School).
            This website is a product of our hard work on a project for the annual event "Hack Tues".
            

            We strive to create an interesting and professional product.
            The key goal of this site is to create an easy-to-use system 
            for health check of the world's brave austronauts around the Universe
             and possibly, for the future generations, residing on Mars.
             

             This project was a blast to make! Especially when done with friends.
             We are very thankful for the opportunity to participate in HackTues Infinity!
             The website was created by a five-man team, with the generous help and attention
             from out dear mentor - Anton Yanchev
